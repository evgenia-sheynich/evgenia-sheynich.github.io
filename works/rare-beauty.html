Rare beauty. Salon appointment solution

Company

Rare beauty is a hairdressing salon, located in LA, California

The problem

The problem is that each time there's an inquiry from the website a manager has to contact back to the potential client to ask specifying questions (like checking the calendar, details about the service/procedure etc) and in total it takes a lot of time to go back and forth.

Task

The idea is to create the UI that would make the process seamless: from the moment of filling the form to the moment when the actual service starts (without any other procedures or questions in between).
